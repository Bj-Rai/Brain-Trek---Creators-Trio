## About the Game
Brain Trek is an adventure and survival-based educational game set in a mysterious forest. The player explores different areas, searches for hidden treasures, and answers subject-based questions to progress in the game.

The game combines learning with gameplay by including questions from different subjects and challenges that require both thinking and decision-making.

---

## Theme
Adventure, Survival, and Education

---

## Subject Integration
The game includes questions and concepts from:
- Science (environment and ecosystem)
- Mathematics (problem-solving)
- Economics (resource management)
- History (general knowledge)

---

## Game Concept
The player moves through a forest by following clues such as checking trees or looking near rocks. When a treasure is found, the player must answer a question to unlock it.

At the same time, the player needs to survive against enemies like snakes and crabs. The player can also meet people in the forest who give small tasks such as planting trees or cleaning waste. Completing these tasks gives rewards.

---

## Learning Objectives
- To understand environmental conservation  
- To improve knowledge in different subjects  
- To develop problem-solving skills  
- To practice decision-making  
- To encourage helping others  

---

## How the Game Works
- The player starts with limited lives (for example, 3 lives)  
- The player explores the forest using clues  
- Finding a treasure leads to a question  
- If the answer is correct, the player earns gold and moves forward  
- If the answer is wrong, the player loses one life  
- Enemies appear in higher levels  
- The player can use weapons to defend themselves  
- Completing side tasks gives rewards  

The game ends when the player loses all lives or completes all levels.

---

## Unique Features
- Combines learning with gameplay  
- Includes real-life related tasks like helping people and protecting the environment  
- Allows players to make decisions during the game  
- Uses a reward system to keep players engaged

## Shop System
Players can use gold to:
- Buy weapons to increase strength  
- Buy hints to help answer questions  
- Unlock skins such as traditional Bhutanese dress
